Privacy Policy for Bye bye ChatGPT popup

Last updated: November 14, 2025

TL;DR: This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or process any user data whatsoever.

Overview

Bye bye ChatGPT popup is a simple Chrome extension designed to automatically close popup dialogs on ChatGPT. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. The extension:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect browsing history
  • Does not track user activity
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

What the Extension Does

The extension performs a single, simple function: it automatically detects and closes popup dialogs on ChatGPT web pages. This action occurs entirely locally in your browser. No information about this action is collected or transmitted.

Permissions

The extension only requests the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Active Tab/Host Permissions: Required to detect and interact with popup elements on ChatGPT pages

These permissions are used solely for the extension's stated functionality and nothing else.

Data Storage

The extension does not store any data locally or remotely. All operations happen in real-time within your browser session.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with, communicate with, or share data with any third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this privacy policy and the "Last updated" date above. However, given the extension's simple nature, we do not anticipate any changes that would involve data collection.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support section.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete privacy while using this extension.
